About the Event

What is Management Carnival?

Management Carnival '26 is K.R. Mangalam University's premier management fest — a platform where students unleash their creativity, sharpen their corporate skills, and compete for glory. Hosted by the DSW & Management Society, this flagship event brings together the brightest minds for a day of innovation, expression, and excellence. Whether you're a creative thinker, a bold presenter, or a passionate content creator — this is your stage.
